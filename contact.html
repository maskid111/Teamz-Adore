Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by browsing our products and clicking the "Order Now" button on any product. This will open WhatsApp where you can send us your order details. Alternatively, you can contact us directly via WhatsApp or email.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including bank transfers, mobile money, and cash on delivery (for select locations). Payment details will be provided when you place your order.

Do you deliver nationwide?

Yes! We deliver to all states in Nigeria. Delivery times and costs may vary depending on your location. Contact us for specific delivery information to your area.

What is your return policy?

We offer returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery, provided the items are in original condition with tags attached. Contact us for return instructions and to initiate the process.

How can I track my order?

Once your order is processed, we'll provide you with tracking information via WhatsApp or email. You can also contact us anytime for order updates.
